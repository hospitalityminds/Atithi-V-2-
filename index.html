About Us

What is Atithiadvisor...?

Atithiadvisor is the brain child of few travel enthusiasts. Atithiadvisor not only allows hosts (like hotels, homestays, campsites etc) to share a feedback of their guests on pre-set parameters...but also opens a whole new world of benefits for the guests who are rated . Sign up for free and start exploring this “other side of the story”

Welcome to Atithi Advisor, the other side of the story. One of its kind platform where you can review your esteemed guest. Not just reviewing but you can make their stay more pleasant by knowing background of your upcoming guests, their personal details, their past stay experience’s written by Host like you. Isn’t it interesting? Registering on AtithiAdvisior is so simple and free. Please join this mission to make Travel industry more interesting and more personalized for the guests and help many host like yours to serve our Atithi better.
